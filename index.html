About Us

ITAI Mining Syndicate (Private) Limited is a newly incorporated Zimbabwean mining company focused on the exploration and development of lithium resources. Formed by ten liberation struggle war veterans with diverse post-war backgrounds, the syndicate unites to pursue opportunities in mining while uplifting local communities. The company holds mining rights to a 62.5-hectare property located 90 km southwest of Mutare, Zimbabwe, comprising two blocks (56.5 Ha + 6 Ha) with demonstrated high-grade lithium mineralization and historical tantalum mining activity.

Our Vision

To become a leading sustainable lithium producer in Zimbabwe, driving economic growth through responsible resource development while creating lasting value for our stakeholders and communities.
